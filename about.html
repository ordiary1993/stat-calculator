About Stat Calculator

Stat Calculator is a free online tool for MMORPG players who want to optimize their character builds.

Features

  • ✓ Calculate all valid stat combinations (STR, AGI, VIT, INT, DEX, LUK)
  • ✓ Support for Standard (1,273 points) and Advanced (1,325 points) builds
  • ✓ Flexible input - fix specific stats or auto-calculate
  • ✓ Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • ✓ Completely free with ad support

Why Use This Tool?

Planning character builds can be time-consuming. This calculator instantly shows you all possible stat combinations that match your requirements, helping you make informed decisions about your character development.
